Program Overview

Digital Ad Expenditure in India was Rs. 9266 Crore Last Year

Learn how you can leverage the power of Digital Marketing with our Digital Marketing Course in Mumbai. This course, vetted by industry experts, aims to make you an expert in 20+ modules that will help you become a highly effective Digital Marketer. These modules are divided into two parts. The first part teaches you execution-related skills that form the core of online marketing. The second part deals with the Managerial Skills which are required to deal with clients. Both are equally important for being a successful online marketer. These skills will be taught to you by professionals who are actually working in the digital space. You will also visit top agencies to understand how the agencies use these skills to run successful online marketing campaigns. With all this knowledge, you will be able to work for some of the top Digital Marketing agencies in the country with our 100% placement assistance. Start your career with the best Digital Marketing Institute in Mumbai. You can do this digital marketing course from our campus which is nearest to you- be it Andheri, Churchgate, Thane or Navi Mumbai.

Live Projects

What are Live Projects?

During the course of the program, every student will be given the opportunity to translate their learnings into the real digital application. They are given live businesses to work on.

How Does It Work?

Students will be responsible to handle the digital marketing of either an existing company/brand or a hypothetical company/ brand provided by IIDE. The faculty will be guiding and assisting the student throughout the entire process.

Benefits of Live Projects?

Before entering the real business world, an IIDE student will already have gotten a first hand experience of the digital universe as compared to his/her peers.
